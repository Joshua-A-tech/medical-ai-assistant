Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we protect your data.

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

MediAI collects minimal personal information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address (when you register)
  • Health Data: Symptoms you enter, diagnosis results
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform
  • Images: Medical images you upload for analysis

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered medical diagnosis
  • Improve our machine learning models
  • Send important updates and health tips (with your consent)
  • Maintain and secure our platform

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. Your data is stored securely and never sold to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of communications

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and platform functionality. No tracking cookies are used.

7. Third-Party Services

We use trusted services for hosting and email delivery. All partners comply with strict privacy standards.

8. Children's Privacy

MediAI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this policy via email or platform notice.

10. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@mediai.com

By using MediAI, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
