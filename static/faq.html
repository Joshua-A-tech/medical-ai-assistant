Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MediAI

What is MediAI and how does it work?
MediAI is an AI-powered medical diagnosis assistant that uses machine learning (97.8% accuracy) to analyze symptoms and provide preliminary health insights. You select your symptoms, and our AI model identifies possible conditions with confidence scores.
Is MediAI a replacement for doctors?
No. MediAI is an educational tool only. It does NOT replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult qualified healthcare providers for medical concerns. In emergencies, contact emergency services immediately.
How accurate is the AI diagnosis?
Our ML model has 97.8% accuracy on test data, trained on 2000+ patient cases with 10 common diseases. However, it's not 100% accurate and should be used as a reference, not a definitive diagnosis.
Is my health data safe and private?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption, never sell your data, and you can request deletion anytime. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
Do I need to create an account?
Basic diagnosis works without an account. However, creating a free account saves your consultation history, allows appointment scheduling, and provides personalized health recommendations.
What symptoms and diseases are covered?
Currently, we cover 60+ symptoms and 10 common diseases including Cold, Flu, COVID-19, Migraine, Gastroenteritis, Allergic Rhinitis, Strep Throat, Bronchitis, Sinusitis, and Pneumonia.
How do I use the medication interaction checker?
Go to the Medications tab, add your medications (one at a time), then click "Check Interactions". The system will identify any dangerous drug interactions from our 150+ interaction database.
Is MediAI free to use?
Yes! MediAI is completely free for all users. We believe healthcare insights should be accessible to everyone.
Can I share my consultation results with my doctor?
Yes! You can export your consultation history or show it directly to your healthcare provider as a reference. However, always rely on professional medical advice.
How can I delete my account and data?
Contact us at privacy@mediai.com with your account email, and we will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.
